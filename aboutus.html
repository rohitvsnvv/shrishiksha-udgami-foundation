About Us

“It is obvious to make a living by what we get, but it is more obvious to make a life by what we give.”


At Shri Sikhsha Udgami Foundation, we deeply contemplate the question, “What are the true responsibilities of humanity?” Our reflection has evolved into action. We believe humanity’s role extends beyond mere existence; it encompasses using our hands to assist others, our feet to reach those in need, and our minds to elevate individuals from dependency to self-sufficiency. Established in 2024 and headquartered in Ajmer, Rajasthan, India, the Shri Sikhsha Udgami Foundation is unwaveringly committed to supporting disadvantaged communities. Every day is an opportunity to advance our vision of creating an “Advantaged Society.” Our mission is to empower economically weaker communities by providing crucial support in areas such as education, food, health, and shelter. Through these efforts, we aim to help individuals reclaim their futures and achieve independence. By focusing on these core areas, we strive to create lasting, positive impacts and foster a society where every person has the chance to thrive and contribute meaningfully to their community.
